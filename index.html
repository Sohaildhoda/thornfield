Our Industry

Chrome Ore Trading

we pride ourselves on our ability to source, market, and trade high-quality chrome ore. Through our established networks and partnerships, we ensure the seamless distribution of chrome ore to meet the global demand, backed by our expert logistical support.


Coal Trading

Our coal trading operations are at the heart of our business, sourcing premium coal from South Africa and Zimbabwe. We provide market insights and manage the supply chain to deliver coal efficiently and sustainably to our international clients.



SME Trading Platform

Understanding the challenges faced by small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in accessing the commodities market, we've developed a trading platform specifically designed for SMEs. This platform offers direct market access and resources, enabling SMEs to participate actively in commodity trading.
